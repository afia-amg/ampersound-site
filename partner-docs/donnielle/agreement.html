2. Purpose

This agreement establishes a reciprocal service exchange partnership between Ampersound Media Group LLC ("AMG") and Donnielle Schroeder / Indigo Reimagined ("Partner"). No monetary compensation is exchanged. Both parties provide professional services of equivalent value.

3. AMG Provides

  • Complete website redesign files (HTML, CSS, JS) ready for developer implementation
  • Developer handoff documentation with full technical specs (colors, typography, animations, responsive breakpoints, file structure)
  • Brand identity concept: "The Ascension Mark" logo in three variants (dark background, light background, standalone icon)
  • Pitch deck for Partner's use in presenting the new brand direction to stakeholders or developers

4. Partner Provides

  • Full bartending services at AMG's Launch Party event at no cost
  • Beverage menu curation for the event (cocktails, mocktails, specialty drinks as appropriate)
  • All necessary supplies, glassware, garnishes, and staffing for beverage service
  • Setup and breakdown of beverage station(s) at the event venue

5. Deliverable Status

  • AMG deliverables: Complete and delivered (accessible via Partner Portal)
  • Partner deliverables: Pending (Launch Party date to be confirmed by AMG)

6. Terms & Conditions

  • This is a one-time service exchange with no monetary compensation on either side
  • AMG deliverables are provided as-is; Partner is responsible for any developer implementation costs
  • AMG will confirm the Launch Party date with minimum 30 days advance notice
  • Both parties agree to cross-promotion on social media and respective partner directories
  • Both parties grant permission to reference this partnership in marketing materials

7. Duration & Termination

This agreement remains active until both parties have fulfilled their respective obligations as described above. Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice via email. If AMG has already delivered services (as is the case), all reciprocal benefits from Partner remain due in full unless mutually agreed otherwise in writing.

8. Liability

Neither party shall be liable to the other for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from this partnership. Each party agrees to represent themselves and their respective brands professionally and in good faith. Partner is responsible for all applicable permits, licenses, and insurance required for beverage service at the Launch Party venue.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Utah.
